# Storyboard Design: tru.atna.ai Deepfake Protection Video
**Tagline:** *"They Couldn't Tell What Was Real. Now You Can."*  
**Target Audience:** Chief Risk Officers (CROs) & Fraud Directors at Banks and Insurance Companies  
**Aesthetic:** Cinematic corporate thriller transitioning to clean, high-confidence fintech teal/mint (#2DD4BF) and white.

---

## 🎨 Brand Identity & Guidelines

- **Primary Colors:** Clean White, Deep Charcoal/Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Accent Color:** Mint Teal (#2DD4BF)
- **Design System:** Sleek, high-contrast, modern fintech interface. Text overlays use clean sans-serif typography (e.g., Plus Jakarta Sans/Outfit).
- **Tone & Mood:** 
  - **Threat Phase (0-30s):** Domestic dread, warm amber changing to cold blue, tense cinematic underscoring, handheld camera movement.
  - **Solution Phase (30-60s):** Confidence, high-tech command center, lateral slides, clean telemetry UI, crisp electronic pulse.

---

## 👥 Character Profiles

### Character A: Margaret (The Victim)
- **Age/Demographics:** 65+, South Asian woman.
- **Visual Style:** Warm, relatable, wearing comfortable homewear in a softly lit study.
- **Narrative Role:** Humanizes the cost of voice and ID deepfakes. Represents the trust that fraudsters exploit.

### Character B: Marcus (The Chief Risk Officer)
- **Age/Demographics:** 52, Black male executive.
- **Visual Style:** Sharp corporate attire (tailored dark blazer, no tie, modern glasses), authoritative yet reassuring.
- **Narrative Role:** The audience’s surrogate. Represents the proactive, modern Risk Officer who shields the bank and its customers using Tru Suite.

---

## 🎬 4-Act Storyboard Structure (60 Seconds)

### 🎬 Unit 01: The Trap Is Set (0–15s)
* **Visuals:** Margaret in her warm-toned home office. She is looking at her phone. A call comes in. The camera moves in a slow push-in. A subtle holographic wave glows around the phone screen (symbolizing synthetic audio).
* **UI Overlay:** Call screen: `Incoming: Private Client Security`.
* **Dialogue / Voiceover (VO):**
  * **5-8s (Synthetic Caller):** *"Mrs. Margaret, this is your bank's security team. We detected unusual activity. Please verify your passcode."*
* **Mood & Palette:** Warm amber tones, domestic setting. Slow, creeping camera movement.
* **Audio:** Quiet hum, sparse piano notes.

### 🎬 Unit 02: The Damage Done (15–30s)
* **Visuals:** The screen transitions from warm amber to a cold, desaturated blue. Margaret is looking at her laptop screen in shock, realizing she's been deceived. Staggered montage: Close-up of her drained account dashboard, followed by rapid-fire shots of anonymous, anxious bank clients.
* **UI Overlay (22-26s):** Text stats overlay: 
  * `CRITICAL EXPOSURE`
  * `$40B+ lost to deepfake fraud in 2024`
* **Dialogue / Voiceover (VO):**
  * **15s (Margaret - Whispering):** *"It was my bank... I trusted the voice."*
* **Mood & Palette:** Cold blues, grey shadows. Tense, low electronic pulse.

### 🎬 Unit 03: The Risk Officer Sees the Pattern (30–45s)
* **Visuals:** Sudden transition. We cut to Marcus in a high-tech bank risk command center. The background is a deep navy blue illuminated by Atna's mint-teal (#2DD4BF) console lights. A digital waveform of an incoming call is active on his primary monitor.
* **UI Overlay (38-43s):** **Tru Suite UI overlay** scanning the waveform.
  * Waveform analysis turns red: `VOICE INTRUSION DETECTED`
  * Confidence meter: `SYNTHETIC VOICE: 97.4%`
  * Action panel: `TRANSACTION FROZEN`
  * Product callout labels: `[Tru Yu: Active]` and `[Tru IDV: Checked]`
* **Dialogue / Voiceover (VO):**
  * **37-40s (Marcus - Commanding):** *"Flag it. Synthetic voice pattern — probability 97%. Freeze the transaction."*
* **Mood & Palette:** Sleek tech command center. Atna Teal (#2DD4BF) and Deep Navy. Confident lateral camera panning.
* **Audio:** High-tech sweep sound effect, electronic transition into a strong corporate beat.

### 🎬 Unit 04: tru.atna.ai: The New Standard (45–60s)
* **Visuals:** Marcus turns to directly address the camera (the CRO viewer). The environment is professional, well-lit, and secure. He stands in front of a clean white presentation panel with Atna branding.
* **UI Overlay (50-54s):** Text overlay: 
  * `Tru Suite: ID, Document, & Voice Authentication`
* **Brand End Card (55-60s):** Clean white screen, black bold typography, and a pulsing teal Atna logo.
  * CTA Text: `Protect your institution. Protect your customers.`
  * Button: `Book a Demo with Our Fraud Specialists`
  * URL: `tru.atna.ai`
* **Dialogue / Voiceover (VO):**
  * **50-54s (Marcus):** *"At tru.atna.ai, we detect what the human ear cannot. We shield your customers and secure your network."*
  * **58-60s (Marcus - Voiceover):** *"tru.atna.ai — Stop fraud before it happens."*
* **Mood & Palette:** Professional, clean, bright white & Atna teal.
* **Audio:** Resolved, positive corporate outro theme.

## 🛠️ Technical Specifications & AI Generation Parameters

| Parameter | Value / Recommendation | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Model** | `kling-v3.0-omni` | Chosen for high-fidelity human expressions and cinematic movement. |
| **Action** | `ref_i2v` (Image to Video) | To maintain character consistency (Margaret and Marcus) and command center environments across shots. |
| **Aspect Ratio** | `16:9` (1920x1080) | Standard format for LinkedIn, executive decks, and corporate presentations. |
| **FPS** | `30` | Smooth presentation playback. |
| **Audio Switch** | `true` | Native audio used for environmental backgrounds, combined with post-production voiceover track. |
| **Total Duration**| `60 seconds` | Structured as exactly 4 generation units of 15 seconds. |

## ⚙️ Product Coverage & UI Elements

We highlight the full **Tru Suite** capabilities to satisfy the Chief of Risk:
1. **Tru Yu:** Voice clone & synthetic voice pattern detection (demonstrated in Unit 03).
2. **Tru IDV:** Deepfake face-swap & physical ID liveness check.
3. **Tru Docs:** Fake document and digital forgery scanning (e.g., manipulated PDF statement checks).
4. **Tru Scribe:** Real-time caller transcript analysis looking for semantic fraud signals.
